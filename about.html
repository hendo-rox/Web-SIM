What Does “FIOLEUR” Mean?

FIOLEUR is a name inspired by the French word fleur, meaning flower, and pain, the French word for bread. While not a direct translation, “FIOLEUR” is a poetic blend we created to evoke the artistry, warmth, and delicate beauty of European baking traditions. The name reflects our passion for creating baked goods that are both elegant and comforting — much like a blooming flower or a perfectly baked loaf. Every item we make is crafted with care, combining fine ingredients, time-honored techniques, and a love for sharing simple, beautiful moments. At FIOLEUR, baking is not just a process — it's a craft, a ritual, and a way to bring joy to the everyday.
